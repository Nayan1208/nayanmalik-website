What is Ubuntu?

Ubuntu is a free and open-source Linux distribution, widely used for personal computers, servers, and cloud computing.

Ubuntu is based on Debian and developed by Canonical Ltd. It comes with a user-friendly interface, robust security, and frequent updates.

Ubuntu supports a vast range of applications and is highly customizable, making it popular among developers and enterprises.
