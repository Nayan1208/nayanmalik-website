What is Ubuntu?

Ubuntu is one of the most popular Linux distributions. It is free, open-source, and widely used for desktops, servers, and cloud computing.

Ubuntu was first released in 2004 and is developed by Canonical. It is based on Debian and provides a user-friendly experience.

Ubuntu is known for its security, stability, and extensive community support. It comes with a variety of pre-installed applications and has a large software repository.

Whether you are a beginner or an advanced user, Ubuntu offers a powerful platform for both personal and professional use.
